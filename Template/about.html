Welcome to Relevant

Relevant Mobile is a loyalty solution that connects brands to customers--on a mission to win hearts, minds and smartphones.

A leading provider of mobile-centric CRM solutions since 2011, we specialize in working with restaurants. Our holistic technology platform offers loyalty, guest feedback, direct marketing and payment solutions to help restaurants better understand and engage with their customers. Using a consultative approach, we partner closely with each client to build a customized, white-labeled app that is a true extension of the brand. Crucially, we tailor core features and architecture, not just colors and buttons.

At the end of the day, our goal is to build apps that build your company--figuring out what works for you, with you, every step of the way.
