Zoey's Kitchen

Take a mother day's photo and share, conquer your wellness targets--LIFE rewards you for more than joining Zoe's for a meal. The rewards are meant to be in the moment with a quick turnaround, so it's fresh for customers and manageable for Zoe. Central to the design is believing in the name, and making an app that attracts people to not just the tastes of the Mediterranean, but to the values that make it great.

Project information
  • Zoey's Kitchen
  • 2013
Work

This is a case study, not a template. Relevant Mobile knows that white-label means molding our offering to your brand, not configuring your identity to match an off-the-shelf shell.

With roots in Alabama and the Mediterranean, Zoe's Kitchen required a strategy that spoke to a sophisticated customer looking for wholesome substance, not sustenance. What resulted from analysis of the brand's goals and customer base was L.I.F.E.

Zoe, meaning Life in Greek, created an app with us that highlights the four value pillars that comprise its identity--Life goals, Inspiration, Food and Enjoyment.

Life goals to set the stage for wellness.

Inspiration to strengthen the proposition by encouraging users to share photos of their progress.

Food and nutrition to measure milestones to keep everyone honest.

Enjoyment to create a positive feedback loop by rewarding the user for accomplishing goals.
